👋 Meet Shubha Baravani, An amazing Associate Professor at Maratha Mandal Engineering College, Belagavi. She joined our college on May 6, 2022 and since then she has been rocking her roles as the Department Electronics & Communications Professor and Institution's Innovation Council Coordinator. 🙌

Shubha Baravani is a brilliant scholar with a Ph.D. in Control and Switch Topology Approach for the Converters used in Wind Energy Conversion System from VTU, Belagavi. She also has a M. Tech. in Digital Electronics from Gogte Institute of Technology, VTU, Belagavi and a B.E. in Electrical & Electronics Engineering from HIT Nidasoshi. She is an expert in signals & systems, digital signal processing, high voltage & power system protection, power generation, transmission & distribution. 🚀

She is also a prolific researcher and innovator who has done many R&D works such as product development, mini hardware project for third year students, and scripting proposals to funding agencies. She has published six journal papers on topics like wind energy conversion systems, matrix converters, and CMOS driver receiver pair. She has also contributed a book chapter on power supply in urban areas using geoinformatics. 📚

Shubha Baravani is not only a smart and talented person, but also a kind and friendly one. She has great interpersonal traits such as honesty, solid commitment to follow through, enterprising & persuasive, problem-solving capability, and team player. She is a life member of ISTE-LM118556 and knows Kannada, Hindi, English, Marathi, and Tulu. She is always ready to help and support her colleagues and students. 😊
