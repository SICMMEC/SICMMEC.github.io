We are TechGeeks, a club of passionate students who want to use our engineering skills to solve real-world problems. Whether it's local or global, we believe that technology and science can make a positive difference in people's lives. That's why we aim to create innovative solutions that address the challenges of our time, such as climate change, health care, education, and more. We also want to inspire others to join us in our mission and to share our enthusiasm for engineering. TechGeeks is more than just a club, it's a community of learners, makers, and problem-solvers.
